I use the _IntelliJ IDEA/ReSharper 2.x_ scheme. This scheme shares the majority of shortcuts with other JetBrains development enviornments. This means I can use the same shortcuts in WebStorm ðŸ‘Œ

## My Top 3 Shortcuts

* Refactor This  `Ctrl`{: .key}+`Shift`{:.key}+`R`{:.key}
  * It's awesome because it will show you the shortcuts available at your cursor position ðŸ”¥)
* Quick fixes / context actions `Alt`{: .key}+`Enter`{:.key}
* Go to everything `Ctrl`{: .key}+`N`{:.key}
  * You can use camel humps for searching. E.g. `MCN` for `MyClassName`
